The Bimble Programming Language

Welcome to Bimble, where innovation meets simplicity. Bimble isn't just another programming language—it's a game changer. With features like automatic error handling and Dot functions, it takes the hassle out of coding and lets you focus on what you do best. Thanks to its strict adherence to BODMAS rules, you can trust that your calculations are always spot-on. And because Bimble is built on Rust, you're not just getting a fast language—you're getting one that's incredibly efficient and reliable. We’ve designed Bimble to be as easy to use as Python, but with even more intuitive features that make coding a breeze. Plus, our unique embedded bytecode generation means your applications are not only fast but also self-contained and portable. Whether you’re working on a simple project or tackling something more complex, Bimble is here to help you take your programming to the next level. Dive in and experience the future of coding today!
